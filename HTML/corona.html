Take Steps to Protect Yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Wearing a medical mask can help limit the spread of some respiratory diseases.Face coverings limit the travel distance of expiratory droplets dispersed when talking.

Wash Your Hands

To prevent the spread of germs during the COVID-19 pandemic, you should also wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds or use a hand sanitizer.

Avoid Close Contact

If you are too close, you can breathe in the droplets, including the COVID-19 virus if the person coughing has the disease. Avoid touching eyes, nose and mouth.

Things Not To Do During Covid

Public transport, metros, gyms and any other crowded place should be avoided. The more the number of people, the more likely of the spread of the virus. If you have symptoms, self-quarantine yourself other than to visit the doctor.

  • Do Not Share Eating
  • Do Not Touch Your Face or Nose
  • Do Not Contact Sick People

Things To Do During Covid

Wear a mask - Whenever you're outside in a public place, you should always wear a facemask. Cover your mouth while coughing and sneezing . You should also cover your mouth with a tissue while coughing or sneezing.
